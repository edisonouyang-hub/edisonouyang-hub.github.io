Brief Manifesto 2026

I study because I want to sing the valor of my friends, new and old, and reveal the glory of my perspective to the world. I wish to preserve history with my art, to provoke people to search for something they have forgotten and to goad them into finding the clues that will lead them to what they are looking for. To do this, I must plan, practice, and continue to push forward but create along the way. An artist that doesn’t make marks will leave nothing behind for the people who may follow them.
