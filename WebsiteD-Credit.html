Brief Manifesto 2026


I am an artist who kinda feels like he's just been drifting around until he finds something to cling onto I cannot promise to be uniquely efficient, I cannot proclaim to be a prodigy. Far from it, I can only speak to what I can offer in cold cut honestly. My work laid out before you is what it is. I suffer for where my faults lay, and I can only strive to retalliate with my own growth. Naturally I identify as someone who would rather embrace imperfection by human hand rather than imperfection by artificial means. It may not be the highest end from the most prestigious soul but you know at least, it came from me—

>

Within the recent years, I have fallen into growth as much as all I feel is that I have only been falling. I am a student of the arts, not the fine arts Not for a long time. I work to be a pillar of my community, but that feels like it is a recent development. I cannot boast, but I can display all my work in the open; not for myself to judge anymore, but for stray eyes who offer to look.
